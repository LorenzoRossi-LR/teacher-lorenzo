Teaching philosophy

Language learning is a cognitive and emotional process, not just a grammatical one. I design lessons around how the mind actually acquires language — drawing on research from Krashen, Vygotsky, and cognitive science — so students make real progress, not just complete exercises.

Who I work with

Adult learners across all levels (A1–C1) — professionals preparing for the workplace, students aiming for Cambridge exams, and anyone who wants to speak with real confidence. 1-to-1 and small group, fully online.
